I am a computer programmer and designer. I love to do both.

In my work, I try to apply the philosophy of the graphic-designer Paul Rand.

A work of art is realized when form and content are indistinguishable. When they are in synthesis. In other words, when they fuse. When form predominates, meaning is blunted [...] When content predominates, interest lags. Paul Rand

I live in Corvallis, Oregon. I design and program websites almost constantly. I am studying Computer Science at Oregon State University.

I love to write for the web. I use HTML5, CSS3 and a lot of JavaScript. I also love to program in Haskell.
